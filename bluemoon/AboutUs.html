About Us


Photography is the pause button of the life

Since 2021 we’ve been proving that you don’t have to be a big company to have a big impact. To this day, we remain a small company of three; we like it that way–it keeps us agile, driven, and humble. We have surrounded ourselves with a diverse group of talented photographers, writers, and advisors who work with us and guide us to build the best product in the business. From creating the first of its kind all-in-one mini session booking solution to simplifying and automating the booking workflow, we make waves and design great software while we do it, and we're just getting started. Today, thousands of customers worldwide use Session to book and manage their sessions, get paid, and grow their business more efficiently and painlessly.
